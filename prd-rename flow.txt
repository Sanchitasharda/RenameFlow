1. Overview
Purpose
RenameFlow is a micro-tool designed to help users rename large batches of files quickly, accurately, and according to strict rule sets. It eliminates the repetitive manual process of renaming files and ensures consistency and accuracy across different file types.
 Ideal for photographers, developers, designers, archivists, and researchers, the tool can handle hundreds of files at once with rule-based automation.
